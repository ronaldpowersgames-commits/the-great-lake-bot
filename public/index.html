🌊 How to use The Lake
🌊 The Basics
💬
Type anything in the box at the bottom. The Lake reflects back what it sees.
📄
Drop a Log — attach transcripts, meeting notes, screenshots, or chat exports. The Lake reads the room.
📷
Take a Photo — snap a whiteboard, document, or screenshot directly into chat.
📎
Multiple attachments — attach several files at once. All will be read and analyzed together.
🎤
Voice input — tap the mic button and speak your thoughts directly into The Lake.
🧠 Reading People & Dynamics
💬
Paste a chat thread — The Lake decodes tone, intent, power plays, and what's really being said between the lines.
🖼️
Drop a screenshot — text message, email, Slack thread. The Lake reads the behavior, not just the words.
🎙️
Paste a transcript — meeting notes, call recordings, interview summaries. The Lake surfaces what the data is really saying.
🔥
Something feels off? Describe the situation. The Lake maps hidden dynamics — who holds power, who's deflecting, what's driving the behavior.
🎯
Before a hard conversation — drop the context and ask The Lake what to watch for, how to position yourself, and what moves are available.
💡 PROMPT SUGGESTIONS

Tap 💡 Prompt Suggestions above the input bar to expand 17 ready-to-use prompts. Every one is a different lens The Lake can use to read your situation.

  • 🧭 Read the Currents — overall real-time read of the situation.
  • 🔍 Surface the Truth — what's really going on beneath the surface.
  • 🪨 Drop the Full Logbook — read everything and give the full picture.
  • 🧩 Connect the Dots — find patterns across people and events.
  • 🔥 Red Flags — what are the red flags? Do not hold back.
  • 🎭 Mask & Motive — what's each person showing vs. what they actually want.
  • 📈 Momentum Check — where is energy building and where is it stalling.
  • 🧊 Iceberg View — what's visible on the surface vs. what's hidden underneath.
  • 🧵 Thread the Story — turn the chaos into a clear narrative.
  • ⚓ Anchor Points — the key facts everything else hangs on.
  • 🌫️ Fog Index — how much clarity vs. confusion and politics is present.
  • 🎯 Intent Scan — what does each person actually want and what are they steering toward.
  • 🧨 Pressure Test — what breaks, who folds, what holds under real pressure.
  • 🪞 Mirror Back — reflect what was just said so it can be seen from the outside.
  • 🌪️ Storm Warning — what's coming if nothing changes.
  • 🧮 Odds & Outcomes — the most likely scenarios and what to bet on.
  • 📌 Followups & Commitments — what was promised, agreed, or left hanging.
🧭 Currents Bar
🧭
The chips at the top are clickable shortcuts — tap any to instantly send that read to The Lake.
🌊
How The Lake Sees You — tap this chip for a full persona read on you, drawn from everything you've ever shared across all your sessions.
⚡
New chips appear automatically as topics emerge in your conversation — each one is a live shortcut back into that thread.
🗂 The Dock
🌊
Past Tides — every conversation saved locally on your device, including forwarded emails.
✏️
Rename a tide — tap the pencil icon on any session to give it a meaningful name.
🗑️
Delete a tide — hover over a session to reveal the trash icon. It only appears on hover so you can't accidentally delete.
🔍
Sonar — search through all your past conversations instantly.
👥 The Crew
⚡
Spotted — The Lake auto-detects names from conversations and suggests them for your Crew.
🪶
Tap + Add to The Crew to manually add someone. Give them a codename, emoji avatar, and context note.
🌊
Tap any crew member → Ask The Lake for a full behavioral read on that person based on everything you've shared.
🎭
Emoji Avatars — assign an emoji to any crew member (e.g. 🦊 cunning, 🧊 cold, 🦈 aggressive). Makes your Crew instantly readable at a glance.
🎯
Codenames — give sensitive people a codename so you can reference them without using real names in the chat.
🎭 Lake Moods
🌊
Calm — reflective, deep, gentle. Best for processing emotions and long-form situations.
🔍
Analytical — structured, precise, systematic. Best for transcripts, data, and decision-making.
⛈️
Stormy — zero filter, raw truth. Use when you need The Lake to stop being polite and just tell you what it sees.
🃏 Score Cards
🃏
Score Cards give you a structured behavioral snapshot — influence, transparency, reliability, and hidden agenda. Each scored out of 10 with a one-line read.
💡
Best used after dropping a transcript, chat thread, or screenshot — the more data The Lake has, the sharper the card.
📊
Tap 🃏 Score Cards in the quick actions bar to generate one from your current conversation.
📧 Email Drop
📨
Forward any email to The Lake — it appears automatically in Past Tides within 30 seconds, ready to analyze.
📋
Forward to:
drop@thelakeapp.app
⚡
Forward email threads, meeting notes, Slack exports, screenshots — anything. Open it from Past Tides and ask The Lake to read it.
🎯
Pro move: Forward a tense email chain → open it in The Lake → ask "What's really going on here and what's my best next move?"
🔒
Emails are stored locally on your device only — they are not stored on any server after delivery.
🌊 How The Lake Sees You
🌊
Tap the "How The Lake Sees You" chip in the Currents bar for a full persona read — drawn from everything you've shared across all your sessions, not just this conversation.
🧠
The Lake will tell you what kind of thinker you are, how you process and communicate, what you consistently focus on, and what patterns it has noticed across all your interactions.
🎯
The more you use The Lake, the sharper this read gets. It learns your style, your concerns, and your blind spots over time.
💡 Pro tip — the more context you give The Lake, the deeper the read. Drop the full thread, not just the headline. The Lake works best when it can see the whole water, not just the surface.
