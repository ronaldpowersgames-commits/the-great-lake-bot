🌊 How to use The Lake
🌊 The Basics
💬
Type anything in the box at the bottom — a situation, a question, a feeling. The Lake reflects back what it sees.
📄
Drop a Log — attach transcripts, meeting notes, or documents and The Lake will read them in full.
🧭
Use the welcome prompts to get started fast if you're not sure what to ask.
🗂 The Dock
🌊
Past Tides — all your saved conversations. Tap any to reload it.
🔍
Sonar — search through your past conversations by keyword or name.
👥
The Crew — track the people in your world. Add context, codenames, and notes.
👥 The Crew
⚡
Spotted — The Lake auto-detects names from conversations and suggests adding them.
🪶
Tap + Add to The Crew to manually add someone with their real name, nickname, codename and context note.
🌊
Tap any crew member → hit Ask The Lake to get a full read on them instantly.
🎭 Moods
😌
Calm — reflective, deep, gentle. Good for processing and thinking out loud.
🎯
Analytical — structured, precise, systematic. Good for mapping situations.
⛈️
Stormy — zero filter, raw truth. Good when you need it straight.
💡 Pro tip — the more context you give The Lake, the deeper the read. Name the people involved, describe the setting, and don't hold back on the details.
